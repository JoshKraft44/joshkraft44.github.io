Antler Addiction

A sustainable business born from a love for nature and craftsmanship.

Antler Addiction started in 2021 as a small project, after collecting naturally shed deer antlers on our family ranch during the COVID-19 pandemic. What began as a hobby grew into a business — turning discarded antlers into meaningful, sustainable products like pet chews and home décor.

Challenges

The journey has never been free from obstacles, like having to quickly learn how to photograph, price, advertise, and dispatch products effectively. As we grew, we faced other challenges: antlers poking through boxes, navigating international wildlife regulations, adapting to sudden changes in U.S. import policies — including a surprise investigation that led to changes in our shipping process, and more.

What I Learned

Through this, I developed skills in e-commerce, professional communication, teamwork, and more. Improving store layouts and shipping logic, learning how to drive traffic through social platforms and SEO, and staying accountable each and every day - all while managing a full course load as a student, has been an incredibly fulfilling pursuit.

Closing Thoughts

What began as a side project during a global shutdown turned into an entrepreneurial journey — one that challenged me, taught me, and continues to evolve. While the logistics and workload were never easy, the process gave me an appreciation for the full arc of building something from scratch.
