Antler Addiction

A sustainable business born from a love for nature and craftsmanship.

Antler Addiction started as a personal hobby during the COVID-19 pandemic. What began as collecting naturally shed deer antlers during long walks at my family cabin quickly turned into a thriving business. Today, we’ve shipped hundreds of orders across three continents, maintaining a 5.0-star average rating from over 200 satisfied customers.
