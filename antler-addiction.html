About the Project

Collecting naturally shed deer antlers during long walks at my family cabin during the COVID-19 pandemic, I saw potential in a growing pile of them. What started as a personal hobby quickly turned into a business opportunity when I realized the potential of these unique pieces for home décor, natural pet chews, and more. Recognizing the demand, I brought my brother Josh on board to help. Together, we have worked to share the beauty and functionality of sustainably sourced antlers while supporting our education and future goals.

Today, we've shipped hundreds of orders across three continents while maintaining a 5.0-star average rating from over 200 satisfied customers. Our commitment to ethical sourcing, premium quality, and sustainability sets us apart. Every antler we sell is 100% natural, cruelty-free, and collected from the wild—never from farms or enclosures.

My Role

Most of my work here is not technical, but sourcing, setting up Etsy, Shopify, communicating with customers, etc. There is some coding done in Shopify's language (Ruby) for basic website improvements and the like.
