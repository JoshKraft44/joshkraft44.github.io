A sustainable business born from a love for nature, shed hunting and entrepreneurial spirit.

Overview

Antler Addiction started in 2021 as a small project, after collecting naturally shed deer antlers on our family ranch during the COVID-19 pandemic. What began as a hobby grew into a business — utilizing unnoticed antlers for purposeful, sustainable products like pet chews and home décor.

Challenges

The journey has never been free from obstacles, like having to learn how to photograph, price, advertise, and dispatch products effectively. As we grew, we faced other challenges: navigating international wildlife regulations, maintaining star seller designation, and more.
